<p><strong>The Encoders for Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Relative Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Encoders for robots come in two main types: Absolute Encoders and Relative Encoders. Absolute Encoders provide a unique digital code for each position, ensuring accuracy and reliability in the robot's movements. On the other hand, Relative Encoders measure position changes relative to a starting point, making them suitable for applications where precise positioning is not required. Both types play a crucial role in enabling robots to perform tasks accurately and efficiently, catering to different needs in the market.</p></p>
