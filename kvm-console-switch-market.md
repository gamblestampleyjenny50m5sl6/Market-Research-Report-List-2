<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>KVM Console Switches are available in two main types - Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type KVM switches use traditional VGA connections to switch between multiple computers, while Digital Type switches use digital connections such as HDMI or DisplayPort for higher video quality. The Analog Type market is more commonly used in older systems that do not have digital capabilities, while the Digital Type market is popular for newer systems with digital video outputs. Both types offer users the convenience of managing multiple computers from a single console.</p></p>
