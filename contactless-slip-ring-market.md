<p><ul><li>Inductive Coupling</li><li>Thermal Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contactless slip rings are devices used to transfer power and data between stationary and rotating parts without the need for physical contact. There are two main types of contactless slip ring technologies: inductive coupling and thermal coupling. Inductive coupling uses electromagnetic fields to transfer power and data, while thermal coupling utilizes temperature differentials to achieve the same purpose. Both technologies offer advantages such as increased reliability, reduced maintenance requirements, and improved efficiency in various applications where traditional slip rings may not be suitable.</p></p>
