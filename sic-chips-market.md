<p><strong>The SiC Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SiC Trench Gate Chip</li><li>SiC Planar Gate Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SiC chips market can be classified into two main types: SiC trench gate chip and SiC planar gate chip. SiC trench gate chips use a vertical trench structure to enhance performance and efficiency, while SiC planar gate chips have a flat design for easier manufacturing and lower cost. Both types offer high power handling capabilities, low power consumption, and high-speed operation, making them ideal for use in power electronics, automotive, renewable energy, and industrial applications.</p></p>
