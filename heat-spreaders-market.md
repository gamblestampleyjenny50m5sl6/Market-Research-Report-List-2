<p><p>Heat spreaders are used in electronic devices to dissipate heat and prevent overheating. T-Wing spreaders have a T-shaped design that helps to evenly distribute heat across a larger surface area, while C-Wing spreaders have a C-shaped design that focuses on directing heat away from specific components. Both types are commonly used in high-performance electronic devices such as computer processors and graphics cards to improve thermal conductivity and maintain optimal operating temperatures. These spreaders play a crucial role in enhancing overall device performance and preventing damage from excessive heat buildup.</p></p>
