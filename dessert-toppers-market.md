<p><p>Dessert toppers market consists of two main types: Edible toppers and Not Edible toppers. Edible toppers are made of ingredients that can be consumed along with the dessert, such as chocolate shavings or sprinkles. They add flavor and texture to the dessert. Not Edible toppers are typically made of materials like plastic or paper and are used for decoration purposes only. They are not meant to be consumed and are usually removed before eating the dessert. Both types cater to different preferences and purposes in the dessert industry.</p></p>
