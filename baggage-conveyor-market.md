<p><p>The Baggage Conveyor market can be divided into two main types: Conveyor Belt and Conveyor Roller. Conveyor belts are continuous loops of material that are used to transport items from one place to another. They are commonly used in airports for moving baggage from check-in counters to luggage carts. On the other hand, conveyor rollers are rotating cylinders that are used to move objects along a path. They are often used in baggage handling systems to smoothly transport luggage between different checkpoints.</p></p>
