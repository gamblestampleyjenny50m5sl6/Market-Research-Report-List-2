<p><p>Smoke Pressure Systems are designed to control smoke movement in buildings during a fire emergency. They come in two main market types: Roof Mounted and Wall Mounted. Roof Mounted systems are installed on the rooftop of a building and are ideal for larger spaces where smoke needs to be extracted efficiently. Wall Mounted systems, on the other hand, are installed on the walls of a building and are more suitable for smaller or confined spaces.
