<p><p>Synthetic lifting slings are commonly used in industries for heavy lifting purposes. The two main types are Round Slings and Web Slings. Round Slings are made of a continuous loop of polyester yarn encased in a protective cover, providing flexibility and durability. Web Slings are made of webbing material, offering strength and load-bearing capacity. Both types are versatile, cost-effective, and easy to use, making them popular choices for lifting operations in various industries worldwide.</p></p>
