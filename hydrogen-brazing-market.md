There are two main types of hydrogen brazing markets - dry hydrogen brazing and wet hydrogen brazing. Dry hydrogen brazing involves the use of pure hydrogen gas without any additional moisture, while wet hydrogen brazing involves the addition of water vapor to the hydrogen gas stream. Both methods are used in various industries for their unique advantages in producing high-quality, strong joints in metal components.</p></p>
