<p><p>EMI gaskets are used to effectively shield electronic devices from electromagnetic interference. There are various types of EMI gaskets available in the market, including wire mesh, rubber, fabric-over-foam, and others. Wire mesh gaskets are made of finely woven wire that provides excellent conductivity. Rubber gaskets offer flexibility and durability while effectively sealing out interference. Fabric-over-foam gaskets combine fabric and foam for a soft, compressible seal. Other types of EMI gaskets may include conductive elastomers or conductive tapes for specific applications.</p></p>
