<p><ul><li>Pixelated</li><li>Coplanar Grid</li><li>Planar-Type</li><li>Quasi-Hemispherical</li><li>Housed Quasi-Hemispherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CdZnTe radiation detectors are used in various applications such as medical imaging, security screening, and nuclear physics research. These detectors come in different types including pixelated, coplanar grid, planar-type, quasi-hemispherical, and housed quasi-hemispherical. </p><p>Pixelated detectors consist of small, individual pixels for high spatial resolution. Coplanar grid detectors have electrodes placed on the same plane for improved charge collection efficiency. Planar-type detectors have a flat surface for easy integration into systems. Quasi-hemispherical detectors have a curved shape for better energy resolution. Housed quasi-hemispherical detectors are quasi-hemispherical detectors enclosed in a protective housing for added durability.</p></p>
