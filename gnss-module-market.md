<p><ul><li>Multi-Constellation</li><li>Multi-Frequency</li><li>Augmentation</li><li>Differential</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS Module Market consists of several types, including Multi-Constellation, which uses signals from multiple satellite constellations for improved accuracy; Multi-Frequency, which utilizes signals from different frequencies to reduce errors and interference; Augmentation, which enhances the accuracy and reliability of GNSS signals through additional data; Differential, which compares a stationary receiver's known position with a mobile receiver's position for improved accuracy; and Others, which includes specialized GNSS modules designed for specific applications. These types cater to various needs in positioning, navigation, and timing technologies.</p></p>
