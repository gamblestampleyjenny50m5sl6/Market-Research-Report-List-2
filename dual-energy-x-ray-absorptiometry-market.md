<p><p>Dual energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) market encompasses two main types of bone densitometers: axial bone densitometers and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers are used to measure bone density in weight-bearing areas of the body such as the spine and hips, while peripheral bone densitometers are used for non-weight-bearing bones like the forearm and heel. Both types of DEXA machines are essential tools in diagnosing osteoporosis and monitoring bone health, playing a crucial role in preventive healthcare and treatment planning.</p></p>
