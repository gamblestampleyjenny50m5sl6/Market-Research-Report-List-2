<p><p>Digital Integrated Passive Device (IPD) market can be divided into two main types: Silicon IPD and Non-Silicon IPD. Silicon IPDs are typically fabricated using silicon-based processes, offering better performance, smaller form factors, and lower cost. On the other hand, Non-Silicon IPDs are manufactured using alternative materials such as glass, ceramics, or polymers, providing different advantages such as better temperature stability, higher reliability, and integration with flexible substrates. Both types cater to different requirements and applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
