<p><ul><li>Digital</li><li>Analog</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal IO modules are devices used to convert signal inputs and outputs from electronic devices to a standardized format for easy communication. The two main types of Universal IO modules are Digital and Analog. Digital modules handle binary signals, which are either on or off, while Analog modules process continuous signals that vary in intensity. Digital modules are commonly used for simple on/off controls, while Analog modules are utilized for tasks requiring precise measurement and control of variables such as temperature, pressure, or voltage.</p></p>
