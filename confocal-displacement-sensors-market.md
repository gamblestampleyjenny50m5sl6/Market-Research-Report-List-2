<p><p>Confocal displacement sensors are used for high-precision measurement applications. The market for these sensors can be divided into two main types: monochromatic confocal sensors and polychromatic confocal sensors. Monochromatic sensors use a single wavelength of light for measurements, offering high resolution and accuracy. On the other hand, polychromatic sensors use multiple wavelengths, providing versatility and the ability to measure different types of surfaces. Both types of sensors cater to specific applications depending on the requirements of the users.</p></p>
