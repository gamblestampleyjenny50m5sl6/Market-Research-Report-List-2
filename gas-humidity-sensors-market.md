<p><p>Gas humidity sensors can be classified into two main types - digital and analog. Digital sensors provide output in the form of discrete numerical values, making them easier to read and incorporate into automated systems. On the other hand, analog sensors provide output in the form of continuous voltage or current signals, which may require additional processing for interpretation. The digital market is growing rapidly due to the ease of use and integration, while the analog market remains relevant for applications requiring precise and real-time data.</p></p>
