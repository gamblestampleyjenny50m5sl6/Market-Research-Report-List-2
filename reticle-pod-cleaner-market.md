<p><p>Reticle POD Cleaner market can be categorized into two main types: EUV POD Cleaner and Non-EUV POD Cleaner. EUV POD Cleaners are specifically designed to clean reticles used in Extreme Ultraviolet lithography, which is a cutting-edge technology in semiconductor manufacturing. Non-EUV POD Cleaners, on the other hand, are used for cleaning reticles in traditional lithography processes. Both types of cleaners are essential in ensuring the quality and reliability of reticles, which are critical components in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
