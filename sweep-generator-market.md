<p><p>Sweep generators are used in various applications to generate different types of sweep signals. Glide sweep generators provide a continuous frequency sweep, while stepped sweep generators change frequency in discrete steps. Time sweep generators sweep over a specific time interval. Other types of sweep generators include frequency modulated (FM) sweep generators and amplitude modulated (AM) sweep generators, which modulate the frequency or amplitude of the signal during the sweep. Each type of sweep generator caters to specific requirements in different industries.</p></p>
