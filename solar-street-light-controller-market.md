<p><ul><li>MPPT Controller</li><li>PWM Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar street light controllers are essential components in ensuring efficient energy management in solar street lighting systems. There are two main types of controllers in the market: MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controller and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) controller. MPPT controllers optimize the power output by continuously tracking the maximum power point of the solar panel, while PWM controllers regulate the voltage and current flowing from the solar panel to the battery. Both types play a crucial role in improving energy efficiency and overall performance of solar street lights.</p></p>
