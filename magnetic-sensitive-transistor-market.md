<p><ul><li>Germanium Magnetotransistor</li><li>Silicon Magneto-Transistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Sensitive Transistor market includes two main types: Germanium Magnetotransistor and Silicon Magneto-Transistor. Germanium Magnetotransistors are made using germanium material and have high sensitivity to magnetic fields, making them ideal for applications requiring precise detection. On the other hand, Silicon Magneto-Transistors are made using silicon material and offer improved performance and durability compared to germanium transistors. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics for sensing and detection purposes.</p></p>
