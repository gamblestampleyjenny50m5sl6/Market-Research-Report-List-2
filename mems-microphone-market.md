<p><p>MEMS Microphones are divided into two main market types: Analog MEMS Microphones and Digital MEMS Microphones. Analog MEMS Microphones convert sound waves into electrical signals, which are then amplified and processed. These microphones are commonly used in applications such as smartphones, tablets, and laptops. On the other hand, Digital MEMS Microphones convert sound waves into a digital signal, eliminating the need for additional analog-to-digital conversion. These microphones are becoming increasingly popular in applications where high-quality audio and noise cancellation are required.</p></p>
