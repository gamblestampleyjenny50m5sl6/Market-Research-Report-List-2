<p><p>The GaN RF Device market is segmented into three main types: GaN-on-Si, GaN-on-SiC, and GaN-on-Diamond. GaN-on-Si devices are cost-effective and widely used for high-power electronic applications. GaN-on-SiC devices offer higher thermal conductivity and power density, making them suitable for radar and aerospace applications. GaN-on-Diamond devices provide the highest thermal conductivity and are ideal for demanding applications such as military and space communications. Each type of GaN RF device caters to different market needs based on performance and cost considerations.</p></p>
