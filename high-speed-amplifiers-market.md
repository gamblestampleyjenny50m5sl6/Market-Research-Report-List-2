<p><p>High Speed Amplifiers are divided into two main types based on their power supply configuration: Single Power Supply and Dual Power Supply. Single Power Supply amplifiers are designed to operate with only one positive power source, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, Dual Power Supply amplifiers utilize both positive and negative power sources, allowing for increased linearity and dynamic range. Each type caters to specific applications and requirements, offering different advantages and trade-offs in performance and design complexity.</p></p>
