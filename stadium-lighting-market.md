<p><strong>The Stadium Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-intensity discharge (HID)</li><li>Light-emitting diode (LED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stadium lighting market can be classified into two main types - high-intensity discharge (HID) and light-emitting diode (LED) lighting. HID lighting systems use gas discharge technology to produce light, offering high levels of brightness but also consuming more energy. On the other hand, LED lighting is more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly, providing excellent illumination while using less power. LED lighting is becoming increasingly popular in stadium lighting applications due to its long lifespan, durability, and low maintenance requirements.</p></p>
