<p><p>Transparency meters are devices used to measure the transparency or opaqueness of materials. There are two main types of transparency meters: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic transparency meters are fully automated and require minimal human intervention. They are typically used for high-volume testing and offer consistent and accurate results. </p><p>On the other hand, semi-automatic transparency meters still require some manual input from operators, such as sample preparation and loading. They are often used in smaller-scale operations where more control and customization are needed.</p></p>
