<p><p>Band pass electronic filters are essential components in electronic circuits which allow only certain frequencies to pass through while blocking others. There are two main types of band pass electronic filters in the market: passive electronic filters and active electronic filters. </p><p>Passive electronic filters rely on passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors to filter out unwanted frequencies. On the other hand, active electronic filters use active components like transistors and op-amps to actively amplify and shape the desired frequencies. Both types of filters have their own advantages and applications in various electronic systems.</p></p>
