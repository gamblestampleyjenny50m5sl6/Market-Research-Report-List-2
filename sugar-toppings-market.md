<p><ul><li>Dry Sugar Toppings</li><li>Wet Sugar Toppings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sugar toppings market can be broadly categorized into two types: dry sugar toppings and wet sugar toppings. Dry sugar toppings include ingredients such as sprinkles, sugar crystals, and powdered sugar that are sprinkled or poured onto various desserts to add sweetness and texture. On the other hand, wet sugar toppings typically consist of syrups, sauces, and caramels that are drizzled or spread over treats like ice cream and pancakes to enhance flavor and moisture. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and culinary applications.</p></p>
