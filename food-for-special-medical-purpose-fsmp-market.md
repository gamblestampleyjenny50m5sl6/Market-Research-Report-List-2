<p><p>Food for Special Medical Purpose (FSMP) market is divided into three types based on their nutritional composition and target conditions. Complete Nutrition FSMPs are designed to provide all necessary nutrients for individuals with specific medical needs. Disease Specific Nutrition FSMPs are formulated to manage conditions such as diabetes, renal failure, or cancer through targeted nutrients. Incomplete Nutrition FSMPs are used in conjunction with a regular diet to supplement specific nutrients that may be lacking due to medical conditions or dietary restrictions. Each type serves a unique purpose in supporting the health of individuals with special medical needs.</p></p>
