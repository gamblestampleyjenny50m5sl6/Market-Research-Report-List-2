<p><ul><li>Guiding catheters</li><li>Guidewires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Minimally Invasive Surgical Guiding Devices market includes guiding catheters and guidewires. Guiding catheters are used to lead medical devices like balloons and stents to specific areas within the body during procedures. They provide support and stability during catheterization. On the other hand, guidewires are thin, flexible wires used to navigate through blood vessels or other pathways within the body to guide catheters or other medical devices. Both of these devices play crucial roles in ensuring precise and successful minimally invasive surgical procedures.</p></p>
