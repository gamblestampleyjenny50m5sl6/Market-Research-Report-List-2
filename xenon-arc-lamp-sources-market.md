<p><p>Xenon arc lamp sources are broadly categorized into three main types based on their output characteristics. Continuous-output Xenon Short-arc Lamps provide steady, high-intensity light suitable for various industrial applications. Continuous-output Xenon Long-arc Lamps offer a longer arc length and higher power output, making them ideal for specialized lighting needs. Xenon Flash Lamps, on the other hand, are designed for high-speed photography and imaging applications where short, intense bursts of light are required. These different types cater to a diverse range of industries and applications.</p></p>
