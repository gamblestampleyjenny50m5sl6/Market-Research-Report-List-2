<p><strong>The Manual Waveguide Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rectangular Waveguide Switches</li><li>Double Ridge Waveguide Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual waveguide switches are commonly classified into two main market types: rectangular waveguide switches and double ridge waveguide switches. Rectangular waveguide switches are designed for rectangular waveguides and typically offer high power handling capabilities. On the other hand, double ridge waveguide switches are specifically designed for double ridge waveguides and provide improved isolation and insertion loss performance. Both market types cater to different waveguide geometries and offer specific benefits depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
