<p><p>Short-range radar is designed for close-range detection and imaging, typically up to 30 meters. It is commonly used in applications like parking assistance and autonomous emergency braking systems. Medium-range radar covers distances up to 60 meters and is utilized in applications like adaptive cruise control. Long-range radar can detect objects up to 200 meters away and is commonly used in applications like highway cruising and advanced driver assistance systems. Each type of radar is optimized for specific ranges and applications to provide accurate and reliable imaging capabilities.</p></p>
