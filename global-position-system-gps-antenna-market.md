<p><p>The Global Position System (GPS) Antenna market is segmented into two main types: Internal Antennas and External Antennas. Internal antennas are built directly into the GPS device and provide a compact and integrated solution. External antennas, on the other hand, are separate from the GPS device and typically offer improved signal reception and accuracy. Both types cater to different customer preferences and applications, with internal antennas being more suitable for handheld devices and external antennas for improved performance in vehicles or outdoor environments.</p></p>
