<p><ul><li>COG Chip</li><li>COF Chip</li><li>COB Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bistable LCD market offers three main types of products - COG Chip, COF Chip, and COB Chip. COG Chip refers to chips on glass, where the driver IC is directly mounted on the glass substrate. COF Chip stands for chip on film, where the IC is mounted on a flexible film instead of the glass substrate. COB Chip, or chip on board, involves mounting the IC directly onto the PCB. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
