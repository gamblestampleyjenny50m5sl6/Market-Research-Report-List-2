<p><p>In the Tyres market, Skewed Tire refers to a type of tire construction where the cords are laid at an angle to the direction of travel, providing good traction on off-road terrains. On the other hand, Radial Tire has cords laid perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better stability and fuel efficiency on paved roads. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with Skewed Tires suited for rough terrains and Radial Tires ideal for everyday driving on smooth surfaces.</p></p>
