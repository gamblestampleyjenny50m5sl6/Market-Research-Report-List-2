<p><p>The Seawater Desalination Device Market consists of three main types: Reverse Osmosis System, Distillation System, and Reverse Osmosis Combined with Distillation System. Reverse Osmosis System uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove salt from seawater. Distillation System involves heating seawater to create steam, which is then cooled and collected as freshwater. Reverse Osmosis Combined with Distillation System combines the two processes to enhance efficiency and produce high-quality freshwater. These technologies play a crucial role in providing clean drinking water in regions facing water scarcity.</p></p>
