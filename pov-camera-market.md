<p><p>POV camera market consists of two main types - CMOS cameras and CCD cameras. CMOS cameras use Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor technology, offering lower power consumption and faster processing speeds. On the other hand, CCD cameras use Charge-Coupled Device technology, providing higher image quality and better light sensitivity. Both types have their own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different applications. The competition between CMOS and CCD cameras drives innovation and advancement in the POV camera market.</p></p>
