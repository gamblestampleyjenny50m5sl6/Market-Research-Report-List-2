<p><strong>The Navigational Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X Band Radar</li><li>S Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X Band Radar and S Band Radar are two types of navigational radars commonly used in maritime applications. X Band Radar operates at a higher frequency (8-12 GHz) and provides better resolution and accuracy for short-range navigation. It is commonly used for small boats, yachts, and on-board systems. On the other hand, S Band Radar operates at a lower frequency (2-4 GHz) and is better suited for long-range detection and tracking of larger vessels. It is commonly used in commercial shipping, military vessels, and offshore platforms.</p></p>
