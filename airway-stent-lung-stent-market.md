<p><p>Airway stents, also known as lung stents, are medical devices used to treat airway obstruction or collapse. There are three main types of airway stents: metal stents, silicone stents, and hybrid stents. Metal stents are made of materials like stainless steel or nitinol and are known for their durability. Silicone stents are flexible and easily customizable, making them suitable for patients with complex airway anatomy. Hybrid stents combine the benefits of both metal and silicone stents, making them versatile and effective in treating different types of airway conditions.</p></p>
