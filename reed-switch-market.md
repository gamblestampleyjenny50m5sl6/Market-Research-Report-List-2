<p><p>A Reed Switch is an electrical switch operated by an applied magnetic field. It consists of two ferromagnetic reeds enclosed in a glass tube filled with inert gas, which become magnetized and attract each other when a magnetic field is applied, completing an electrical circuit. Reed switches are commonly used in various applications such as sensors, relay control, and security systems due to their simple construction and long operational life.</p><p>The Reed Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. <p><p>Reed switches come in different market types based on their functioning and design. Form A reed switches are normally open when there is no magnetic field present, while Form B switches are normally closed in the absence of a magnetic field. Form C switches have one normally closed and one normally open contact. Other market types may include latching reed switches, high-power reed switches, and sensor reed switches. These variations cater to different applications and industries, providing flexibility and versatility in their usage.</p></p>
