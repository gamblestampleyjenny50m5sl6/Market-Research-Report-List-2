<p><p>Magnetic proximity switches are devices used to detect the presence of a magnetic field and trigger a response. Reed type switches utilize reed switches that are actuated by a magnet, providing a reliable and cost-effective solution. Hall effect type switches use solid-state technology to sense magnetic fields and generate a digital output signal. Both types are commonly used in various industries for position sensing, door and gate control, and security applications. The market for these switches is expected to grow due to increasing demand for automation and IoT integration in different sectors.</p></p>
