<p><p>Boiler tube leak detectors are available in two main types: Airborne Sensor and Structure Borne Sensor. Airborne sensors detect leaks by monitoring the acoustic signals in the air, while structure borne sensors detect leaks by detecting vibrations in the structure itself. Both types of detectors are essential for identifying and locating leaks in boiler tubes, ensuring the safety and efficiency of the boiler system. Customers can choose the appropriate detector based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
