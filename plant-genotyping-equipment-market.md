<p><p>Plant genotyping equipment market includes various types such as SNP genotyping equipment, real-time PCR instruments, DNA analyzers, liquid handlers, and others. SNP genotyping equipment is used for identifying single nucleotide polymorphisms in plant genomes. Real-time PCR instruments measure amplification of DNA during PCR reactions. DNA analyzers are used for analyzing DNA sequences. Liquid handlers automate the process of dispensing liquids in genotyping assays. Other types of equipment may include microarrays, sequencers, and gel electrophoresis systems in plant genotyping.</p></p>
