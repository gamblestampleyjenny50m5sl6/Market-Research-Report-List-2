<p><p>Rotary Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) can be categorized into two main types: single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase Rotary UPS is designed for smaller applications where a single-phase power supply is sufficient, such as residential homes or small businesses. On the other hand, Three-phase Rotary UPS is suitable for larger industrial or commercial settings where a three-phase power supply is required for high-power equipment. Both types of UPS systems provide backup power in case of electrical outages, ensuring continuous and reliable power supply.</p></p>
