<p><p>Stand alone smoke detectors are devices used to detect smoke and emit a loud alarm to alert individuals of a potential fire hazard. There are two main types of stand alone smoke detectors - Ionization Smoke Detectors and Photoelectric Smoke Detectors. Ionization smoke detectors are more responsive to flaming fires, while photoelectric smoke detectors are more sensitive to smoldering fires. Both types are important for fire safety in homes and buildings, as they provide early warning and can potentially save lives.</p></p>
