<p><p>Single diodes in the Silicon Carbide (SiC) market refer to diodes that have only one PN junction, allowing current to flow in one direction. These diodes are commonly used in high voltage and high temperature environments due to their superior performance compared to traditional silicon diodes. </p><p>Dual diodes, on the other hand, consist of two PN junctions in a single package, providing increased efficiency and reliability. They are often used in applications where redundancy and higher power handling capabilities are required.</p></p>
