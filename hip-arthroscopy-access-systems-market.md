<p><ul><li>Pincer Type</li><li>Cam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hip arthroscopy access systems are categorized into two main types: Pincer Type and Cam Type. Pincer Type access systems are designed to treat hip impingement caused by an overgrowth on the acetabular rim, while Cam Type access systems are used to address impingement caused by a misshapen femoral head. Both types of access systems are crucial in hip arthroscopy procedures for providing clear and safe access to the hip joint, allowing surgeons to accurately diagnose and treat various hip conditions.</p></p>
