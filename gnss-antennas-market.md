<p><p>GNSS antennas can be categorized into two main types: internal and external. Internal antennas are typically smaller in size and are integrated within the device, such as in smartphones or tablets. They provide accurate positioning while being compact and convenient. On the other hand, external antennas are larger and are mounted outside of the device, usually on a vehicle or a building. They offer better signal reception and are preferred in situations where a strong and consistent signal is necessary.</p></p>
