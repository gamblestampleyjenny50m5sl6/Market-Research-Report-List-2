<p><p>GNSS boards are used in various applications such as navigation, surveying, mapping, and timing. The market for GNSS boards can be divided into single-frequency and multi-frequency types. Single-frequency boards receive signals from one frequency band, while multi-frequency boards are capable of receiving signals from multiple frequency bands simultaneously. Multi-frequency boards typically offer higher accuracy and reliability compared to single-frequency boards, making them suitable for more precise applications such as surveying and geodetic mapping.</p></p>
