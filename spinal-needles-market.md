The ≤38mm needles are typically used for pediatric patients or for procedures requiring shallow penetration. The 38-76mm needles are commonly used for standard lumbar punctures and spinal injections. The ≥76mm needles are used for obese patients or for procedures requiring deeper penetration. The choice of needle length depends on the specific procedure and patient population, ensuring safe and effective delivery of medications or fluids into the spinal space.</p></p>
