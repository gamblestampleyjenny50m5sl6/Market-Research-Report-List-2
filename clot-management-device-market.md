<p><strong>The Clot Management Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inferior Vena Cava Filters(IVCF)</li><li>Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices</li><li>Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis(CDT) Devices</li><li>Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Embolectomy Balloon Catheters/Clot Management Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clot Management Device Market includes various types of devices used for managing blood clots in different parts of the body. Inferior Vena Cava Filters (IVCF) are used to prevent blood clots from traveling to the lungs. Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices are used to remove clots in the brain or spine. Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis (CDT) Devices dissolve clots using medication. Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices physically remove clots from blood vessels. Embolectomy Balloon Catheters/Clot Management Catheters are used to break up and remove clots in the blood vessels.</p></p>
