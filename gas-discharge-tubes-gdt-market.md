<p><p>A Gas Discharge Tube (GDT) is a type of gas-filled surge protection device that is used to protect electronic equipment from voltage spikes and surges. GDTs work by creating a low-resistance path for the surge to follow, thereby diverting it away from sensitive electronics. They are commonly used in applications such as telecommunications, power supply networks, and industrial equipment.</p><p>The Gas Discharge Tubes GDT Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period.
