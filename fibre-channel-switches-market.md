<p><strong>The Fibre Channel Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modular Director Switches(Backbone Switches)</li><li>Semi-modular Switches(Edge Switches)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modular Director Switches, also known as Backbone Switches, are typically used in larger networks where high port count and scalability are needed. These switches feature a modular design with hot-swappable components for easy maintenance and upgrades. On the other hand, Semi-modular Switches, or Edge Switches, are used in smaller environments where a lower port count is sufficient. They offer a more compact and cost-effective solution compared to Director switches while still providing essential Fibre Channel switching functionality.</p></p>
