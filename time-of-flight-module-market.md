<p><p>A Time of Flight (TOF) Module is a type of sensor that uses infrared light to measure the distance between the sensor and an object by calculating the time it takes for the light to reflect off the object and return to the sensor. These modules are commonly used in various applications such as robotics, automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics for tasks like object detection, gesture recognition, and collision avoidance.</p><p>The TOF Module Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in sensor technology and the development of more compact and cost-effective TOF modules are also driving market growth.</p><p>Latest trends in the TOF Module Market include the integration of TOF sensors with other technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance the capabilities of these modules.
