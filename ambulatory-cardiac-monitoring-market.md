<p><ul><li>Holter Monitor</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring includes different types of devices to continuously track and record the heart's electrical activity outside of a clinical setting. Holter monitors are portable devices worn by patients to record heart rhythms for 24-48 hours. Event monitors are used for longer periods to capture intermittent cardiac events. Mobile cardiac telemetry devices provide real-time monitoring and transmission of data to healthcare providers. Other types of ambulatory cardiac monitoring may include patch monitors or implantable loop recorders for more specific monitoring needs.</p></p>
