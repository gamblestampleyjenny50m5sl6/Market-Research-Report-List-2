<p><p>Grid Single Phase Micro Inverter market refers to inverters that are connected to the electricity grid for power supply. These inverters convert the DC power from solar panels into AC power to be used in homes or businesses. On the other hand, Off Grid Single Phase Micro Inverters are standalone systems that are not connected to the grid. They store excess energy in batteries for use when there is no sunlight. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
