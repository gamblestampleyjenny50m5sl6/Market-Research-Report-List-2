<p><p>Blow molding machines are categorized into different types based on their structures and functionalities. The Extrusion Blow Molding Machine is commonly used for producing hollow plastic objects by extruding a tube of molten plastic and then blowing air into it. The Special Structure Blow Molding Machine is designed for specific manufacturing requirements. The Injection Blow Molding Machine combines injection molding and blow molding processes. Other types of blow molding machines include continuous and reheat stretch blow molding machines tailored for specific production needs.</p></p>
