<p><p>Industrial smart cameras are used in various applications, with the two main types being area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture an entire image in a single frame, making them ideal for inspecting stationary objects or capturing detailed images. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture images line by line, making them suitable for inspecting moving objects or continuous processes. Both types of cameras play a crucial role in industrial automation, quality control, and surveillance systems.</p></p>
