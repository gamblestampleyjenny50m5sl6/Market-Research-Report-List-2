<p><ul><li>Batch Fryers</li><li>Continuous Fryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial frying machines can be categorized into two main types - batch fryers and continuous fryers. Batch fryers are designed to fry a set amount of product at a time, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, continuous fryers are ideal for high-volume production as they can fry products continuously without needing to stop for reloading. Both types of fryers have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as food processing, snacks manufacturing, and restaurants.</p></p>
