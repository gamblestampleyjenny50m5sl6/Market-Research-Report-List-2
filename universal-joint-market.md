<p><strong>The Universal Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Velocity Joint</li><li>Cross-axis Universal Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Constant Velocity Joint (CVJ) is a type of universal joint that maintains a constant velocity rotation between two shafts and is commonly used in front-wheel drive vehicles. On the other hand, Cross-axis Universal Joint allows for the transfer of torque between shafts that are not aligned. While CVJ is ideal for smooth and efficient power transmission, Cross-axis Universal Joint is more versatile in accommodating angular misalignment.Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
